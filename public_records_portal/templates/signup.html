We are building a new web application that will help cities manage and respond to public records requests. It also gives the public a quick, easy way to submit public records requests.

Anyone can view every uploaded document and message submitted through this application. This allows the public and city employees to search through past requests, view previously released records, and better understand what happens to each request.

If you are interested in reusing this tool we'd love to connect. Just fill out the form to the right.
